# Keyword Research — Instructions Detail

## Phase 1: Scope

Ask if missing: product/service, audience, business goal, market, language, current site URL, known metrics.

## Phase 2: Discover seeds

Categories: core terms, problems, solutions, audience labels, industry jargon, brand adjacents.

## Phase 3: Expand

Patterns: `{seed} + how/to/best/vs/cost/near me` · questions · year modifiers · "for {audience}"

## Phase 4: Intent classification

See [keyword-intent-taxonomy.md](keyword-intent-taxonomy.md).

## Phase 5: Scoring

| Difficulty | DR proxy | SERP signals |
|------------|----------|--------------|
| Low | Mixed results, forums | |
| Med | Brands, guides | |
| High | Major brands, tools | |

Opportunity = (Volume × IntentValue) / max(Difficulty,1). IntentValue: info=1, nav=1, commercial=2, transactional=3.

## Phase 6: AEO flags

Question queries, definitions, steps, comparisons → flag for `answer-content-optimizer`.

## Phase 7: GEO flags

Definitional, statistical, "what is X" → flag for `ai-friendly-content-optimizer`.

## Phase 8: Deliver

Sections: Executive Summary · Quick Wins (≤3) · Growth targets · AEO/GEO targets · Topic clusters · 30-day calendar · Handoff.

## Actionable vs generic

❌ "Target long-tail keywords"  
✅ "`best crm for freelancers` — Estimated vol 880, difficulty 22, commercial, comparison page"
